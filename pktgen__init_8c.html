network_init_ctm

Initialize CTM for network traffic (receive or transmit)

Parameters
islandIsland number of CTM to initialize
mem_for_pkts0 to use all CTM for packets, 1 for 1/2

On later devices a value of mem_for_pkts of 2 and 5 are one or three quarters and 3, 4, 6, 7 are one, seven, five and three eights respectively

The CTM is assumed to have been correctly reset (i.e. its packet work queue is already empty)
